Final Fantasy hooked me on RPGs

I'd played games before FF. I know I'd played in arcades and there were games there that hooked me. I'd had older consoles (Atari 2600 anyone). But it wasn't until Final Fantasy that I found the genre of games that would keep me hooked for decades.
